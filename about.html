About Us

The Computer Science at Stanford University (CSSU) is a council aimed to strengthen the CS community at Stanford, enhance the experience of our university life, and create long-term traditions in the CS community at Stanford. The student council serves CS undergraduates, CS master's students, and those with an interest in CS at Stanford.

The student council serves students in four aspects: academic, social, internal relations, and external relations.

Academia

Facilitate students in exchanging the latest technological innovations, and foster the formation of collaborations in research or entrepreneurships.

Social Activities

Provide opportunities for peers to connect with each other, with the faculty and other departments at Stanford.

Internal Relations

Assist the council in the liason with the CS Department, Graduate Student Council, the Office of Student Engagement and all other organizations within the University.

External Relations

Collaborate with CS student organizations at other universities.

Freshman Outreach

Provide help to students who wish to learn more about the CS major and pursue CS.
